Cognitive Science Bayesian Modeling
Investigating Emotions via Bayesian Models over Monetary Outcomes

This project uses probabilistic programming to infer emotional responses based on financial rewards in a wheel-spin game. Models were built for 8 distinct emotion categories, accounting for both general patterns and individual variability. Developed as part of a Master’s thesis in Cognitive Science.
