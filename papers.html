Positive Emotion Recognition—A Survey of Computational Models
Irem Arici, Radoslaw Niewiadomski
IEEE Transactions on Affective Computing (or relevant journal), 2024
Survey Affective Computing Machine Learning
Abstract

We provide a systematic survey of works on positive emotion recognition and detection. We queried major research paper databases for 12 different emotional states. From an initial pool of more than 800 papers, we selected 81 that propose recognition models. As the first survey to focus on a large number of distinct positive emotions, our paper contributes to Affective Computing by demonstrating the unexplored potential and identifying significant shortcomings in current datasets.
